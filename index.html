How PassAudit protects your privacy
01

Local hashing. Your password is converted to a SHA-1 hash entirely inside your browser. It never leaves your device in plain text.

02

k-Anonymity model. Only the first 5 characters of your 40-character hash are sent to the Have I Been Pwned API — mathematically impossible to reverse.

03

Local comparison. The API returns thousands of matching fragments. Your browser checks them locally — the server never learns your full hash.

04

Instant audit. You get breach count, strength grade, entropy score, crack time estimate — and a downloadable report.
